LifePath Generator

This is my version of another lifepath generator. Choose a source before beginning. Most of the sourcebooks that contain some part of lifepath generation have been included.
Click on GENERATE to have randomly selected entries in that section.
For life events, enter the number of events to generate in the box and then click GENERATE. If you enter a number less than 1 or a non-numerical value, then a value between 2-12 will be generated.
