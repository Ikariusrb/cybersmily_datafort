Cyberpunk Red Jumpstart Kit Quick Pre-generator Characters

This utility is for creating a random PC/NPC based on Cyberpunk Red Jumpstart Kit templates. The character the gets generate will have the random name, role, stats, and lifepath as per the jumpstart kit templates. You can print this sheet, currently there is no save mechanism.

You can purchase Cyberpunk Red Jumpstart Kit from R. Talsorian Games here or the PDF from Drive Thru RPG here.

Click on Generate button below to get started.
