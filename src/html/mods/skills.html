Here's a list of skills a player would use, and the appropriate skill/DIFF to oppose it. Also I'll list a couple of suggestion on when the skill might be needed.
A Ref has the final word on this.
The Ref could judge that you might be able to use Perform instead of Fast Talk & Persuasion in a situation. But I would apply a +5 modifier because you're not using the right skill, but one that is similar. This is my opinion, if a Referee feels that it doesn't make sense. Then don't use it.
I believe the basic book should have had a similar list in it to help new Refs. There are some opposing rolls that require the Ref to make a number. Just use common sense. I suggest that a Ref read through the skills and become familiar with what they can do.
I would also require a skill roll if the player is trying something that their skill level is lower than what is required i.e. Gamble "At +6 you can make a living at the tables in Vegas or Monte Carlo". If the player had a +2 and wanted to try making a living, I would require a roll for almost every hand. If he had a +6, I would require one if he wanted to make more than just a living (living is food, drinks and shelter, so about 100-200eb a day).
And finally, this is going to be a work in progress type of thing. I hope to complete most of it, but who knows where my mind shall wander.

Strength Feat
Opposing Skill/DIFF
Strength Feat
Throw
DIFF set by Ref
Additional Info/Descript
The DIFF depends on what the player is trying to do. Busting down a wooden door w/ no thrills is probably a 10-15DIFF. A steel reinforced door, at least 30+.
If an opponent is trying to throw the player, I would allow Str Feat (if they have it) to resist, but with a +5 penalty.

Oratory
Opposing Skill/DIFF
Ref sets DIFF
Additional Info/Descript
Oratory should be used when you're talking to a crowd. The lower the roll, the less likely people are going to be paying attention or caring about what you have to say. A player could use it to Fast Talk a crowd, but should have a higher DIFF.
Resist Torture/Drugs
Opposing Skill/DIFF
Interrogation
Expert: Torture
DIFF set by Ref
Additional Info/Descript
To resist a drug you just roll against the strength. Some Ref might require a roll ever turn for the drug's duration
Torture is a different matter. It all depends on the severity. A cigarette burn to the arm, DIFF 5. A red hot coil to the face, DIFF 25+.
